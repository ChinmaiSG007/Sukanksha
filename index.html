About

VISION AND MISSION

VISION

To create a compassionate and inclusive society where every abandoned child, orphan, and elderly individual is provided with love, care, and the opportunity to live a life of dignity and fulfillment.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide safe shelter, nutritious food, educational support, and emotional care to abandoned children, orphans, and senior citizens. We aim to empower children with skills for a brighter future while ensuring that the elderly live their remaining years with respect and care.

Problem statement(s) the organisation is addressing

In India, a large number of children are abandoned or orphaned, while many elderly individuals are left without support. These individuals lack access to basic necessities, including food, shelter, and education, and are often marginalized in society. The Sukanksha Charitable Trust seeks to address these critical gaps, providing a nurturing environment for these vulnerable groups.

PROJECTS

Project names, project goals, project description, project impact (no of senior citizens/ students, their age groups)

Under the umbrella of Sukanksha Madilu, we have three core projects that focus on providing a safe and caring environment for children and the elderly:

Sukanksha Girls' Home

  • Goal:
    To provide a safe and nurturing environment for abandoned and orphaned girls.
  • Description:
    The Sukanksha Girls' Home shelters 27 girls aged 6 to 16, offering them food, shelter, and access to education.
  • Impact:
    27 girls are currently being supported, receiving education and care to grow into independent young women.

Sukanksha Boys' Home

  • Goal:
    To offer shelter and educational opportunities to abandoned and orphaned boys.
  • Description:
    The Sukanksha Boys' Home houses 15 boys aged 10 to 16, providing them with basic necessities and educational support.
  • Impact:
    15 boys are currently benefiting from this project, being guided toward a brighter future.

Sukanksha Old Age Home

  • Goal:
    To provide care and support for senior citizens without family or means of support.
  • Description:
    Our old age home provides 20 elderly individuals with food, shelter, and medical care.
  • Impact:
    20 senior citizens are currently being supported, living in a community where they are cared for and respected.

Sukhanksha Ranga Madilu

  • Goal:
    To introduce drama and performance arts to students as a means of skill development and creative expression.
  • Description:
    Through this initiative, we aim to provide students with opportunities to explore drama and acting, encouraging creativity and communication skills. Workshops and training sessions are organized to nurture their talents.
  • Impact:
    This project is in the early stages, and we are working to involve children from both the boys’ and girls’ homes to foster artistic growth.
